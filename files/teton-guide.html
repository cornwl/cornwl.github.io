Getting There

JAC is inside the park

Jackson Hole Airport (JAC) is the only commercial airport inside a U.S. national park — you'll see the Tetons from the runway. The drive to Heartwood Lodge in Teton Village is about 14 miles (~20–25 min). You'll want rental vehicles for this trip: the lodge has garage parking for 2 cars and driveway space for 4 more (no street parking per HOA).

Rental car note With 18 guests you'll need multiple vehicles. Reserve well ahead — Jackson Hole car rental inventory is extremely tight in peak summer and prices surge. Consider booking through Costco Travel, Turo, or directly with National/Enterprise at JAC. Two large SUVs + one midsize could work.
Parking rules — HOA enforced Garage: 2 cars. Driveway: up to 4 cars. No street parking, no RVs, no sprinter vans, no trailers, no motorcycles. Fines are charged to guests.

Weather & What to Pack

Layer up

Late June / early July is prime Teton summer — long sunny days, wildflowers everywhere, and afternoon thunderstorms that roll in fast and clear out within an hour. Mornings can be genuinely cold (mid-30s°F), afternoons warm (upper 70s–low 80s°F), and evenings cool off quickly after sunset around 9 PM.

EveryoneLayers: morning fleece + afternoon t-shirt. Rain jacket (not optional). Sunscreen SPF 50+. Sunglasses. Water bottles. Comfortable walking shoes.
Activity daysClosed-toe shoes (required for horseback, UTV, rafting). Long pants for horseback ride. Quick-dry clothes for rafting. Hat for sun.
EveningsLight jacket or hoodie — rooftop dinners and the rodeo get chilly. Layers for the covered wagon ride up Cache Creek Canyon.
KidsSun hats, warm layers for mornings, snacks for car rides. Bear spray is for adults only (available at Moose Junction or Jackson).

Teton Village & Surroundings

Heartwood Lodge sits atop Granite Ridge in Teton Village, which is a self-contained base at the foot of Jackson Hole Mountain Resort. The village has restaurants, shops, and the aerial tram (runs in summer — ride it for incredible views). The town of Jackson is about 12 miles south (~20 min). Grand Teton National Park's main corridor runs right past you.

Fine dining at 7,808 ft. Regional cuisine by Chef Brandon Hicks (ex-Michelin-starred Picholine). Elk tenderloin, bison, seasonal plates, craft cocktails. Tri-level: bar, dining room, outdoor deck + rooftop patio. Accessible by scenic gondola only. 24-ft LED mural behind the bar.

Airport notes
July 4th airport warning JAC is a small airport and July 4th is one of the busiest travel days of the year in Jackson Hole. Arrive 2+ hours early. TSA lines can be long.
